## GEO vs Traditional SEO

### Traditional SEO (Declining)
- **Goal:** Rank #1 on Google for keywords
- **Tactics:** Backlinks, keyword density, page speed
- **User journey:** Search → SERP → Click → Website
- **Monetization:** On-site ads, affiliate links after click

### GEO (Growing)
- **Goal:** Get cited by AI as authoritative source
- **Tactics:** Structured data, depth, EEAT signals, citations
- **User journey:** Ask AI → Get answer → (Maybe) click source
- **Monetization:** Be the recommended product/service in AI answer

---

## What AI Search Engines Prefer

### Content Characteristics
1. **Long-form depth:** 3000+ words (2.5x better than short)
2. **Structured data:** Schema markup, clear hierarchies
3. **Citations:** Hyperlinked sources, attribution chains
4. **Clarity:** FAQ format, explicit relationships
5. **Authority:** EEAT (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust)
6. **Multimedia:** Video (30% of AI Overviews), annotated images
7. **Freshness:** Regular updates signal reliability

### Content That Gets Cited
- **Product comparisons** with clear data
- **How-to guides** with step-by-step structure
- **Research-backed articles** with sources
- **Tools/resource directories** with metadata
- **Industry insights** from real experience

### Content AI Ignores
- Thin affiliate spam
- Pure AI slop (no human touch)
- Keyword-stuffed garbage
- Sites with no authority signals
- Shallow, surface-level content

## Affiliate Marketing in AI Era

### Old Model (Breaking Down)
1. Create SEO content
2. Rank on Google
3. User clicks
4. User sees affiliate link on your site
5. User clicks affiliate link
6. You get commission

### New Model (Emerging)
1. Create AI-optimized content
2. AI cites you as source
3. User asks AI "What's the best X?"
4. AI says "According to [your site], Product Y is best because..."
5. AI includes your affiliate link OR user clicks through
6. You get commission

**Challenge:** AI might summarize without sending clicks  
**Opportunity:** If you're THE cited source, you own the recommendation

## Risks & Unknowns

### Risk 1: Zero-Click Problem
- AI answers question without sending traffic
- You get cited but no clicks = no revenue
- **Mitigation:** Be THE source AI trusts, own the recommendation

### Risk 2: Algorithm Changes
- Just like Google Panda/Penguin hurt SEO spammers
- AI could change what it prefers
- **Mitigation:** Focus on genuine quality, diversify across AI platforms

### Risk 3: AI Spam Detection
- Pure AI-generated slop gets filtered
- Need human authenticity + depth
- **Mitigation:** Blend AI efficiency with human expertise

### Risk 4: Conversion Rates Unknown
- No clear data on AI-driven affiliate conversions yet
- Might be lower than traditional click-through
- **Mitigation:** Test and measure, optimize over time

### Risk 5: Market Timing
- Could be too early (low AI search volume still)
- Could be too late (saturation already happening)
- **Data suggests:** Sweet spot NOW (25% volume, growing fast)
